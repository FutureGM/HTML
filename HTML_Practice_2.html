Making a PB and Bannana Sandwich

  1. Grab 2 slices of bread (rye or wheat is healther!)
  2. Place bread on a clean,flat surface
  3. Grab Peanut Butter Jar, butter knife and 1 Bannana
  4. With knife, scoop a heaping amount of Peanut Butter
  5. Spread onto each bread slice, evenly, until smooth
  6. Slicy bannanas
  7. Place bannana slices on bread slices
  8. Enjoy!
